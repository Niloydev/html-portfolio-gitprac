The Best Anime According to Niloy

My top 3 Animes of all time.

Naruto

This a anime based on a boy who became a savior of the world even after facing so many obstacles and childhood trauma.

One Piece

This is a ongoing anime of a boy who dreamt to become the king of all pirates of the world.

Demon Slayer

The hero of this anime lost all his family excluding his sister on a demon attack. His sister became a demon because that attack so the hero decided turn his demon sister to normal human being.
